LEE COOPER X TEALER
From London to Paris, Lee Cooper to Tealer. Lee Cooper joins forces with
the rising star of Parisian streetwear to break the codes with a limited-
edition collection of modern jeans.
Lee Cooper x Tealer is now available exclusively in France at the Tealer Paris boutique and online. Bringing
together the best of Parisian streetwear and British denim.
The iconic Lee Cooper jeans are decorated with the street attributes of the protagonist Tealer. This
unexpected marriage between the East End of London and the streets of Paris is sealed with the design of
this collection composed of three denim designs available in black, blue and light blue.
Find out more on the Lee Cooper France website.
